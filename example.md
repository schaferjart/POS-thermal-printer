# Ephemeral

Lasting for a very short time. From Greek *ephemeros*, meaning 'lasting only a day'. Used to describe things that are **transitory**, fleeting, or momentary in nature.

- The ephemeral beauty of cherry blossoms has inspired Japanese poetry for centuries.
- All fame is ephemeral. — **Voltaire**

---

> To see a world in a grain of sand, and a heaven in a wild flower, hold infinity in the palm of your hand, and eternity in an hour.
